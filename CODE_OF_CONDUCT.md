# Code of Conduct

This project adapts the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/) v2.1.

## Our Pledge
We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for
everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity
and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race,
religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards
Be respectful, inclusive, and professional. Examples of unacceptable behavior:
harassment, insulting or derogatory comments, personal or political attacks,
publishing others' private information, and other conduct that is inappropriate
in a professional setting.

## Scope
This Code applies within all project spaces and when an individual represents
the project in public spaces.

## Enforcement
Report conduct concerns privately to the maintainers (see SECURITY.md for the
private reporting channel). Maintainers will review and respond, and may remove,
edit, or reject contributions and contributors that violate this Code.
